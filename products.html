The Problem

The problem we face is the unnecessary waste created by littering. Everywhere you look you see litter in one form or another. For example, you can find hundreds of cigarette butts on the ground. You can see small pieces of waste on the side walks. This garbage and waste make people feel uncomfortable and want to stay outside. Many people have tried to solve this problem through things like public waste bins (recycling bins, garbage bins, compost bins), picking up litter, and reusing products. Unfortunately, public waste bins are not everywhere and it still leaves space for people to litter. When picking up litter you may be discouraged by the effort it takes, some may even be disgusted by the litter. Also many people don't think about reusing products like coffee cups and plastic waste and end up dropping it. We want to solve this problem and keep our cities as clean as possible.
